Pesto Pasta


Description

Italian pasta with pesto sauce

Ingredients

  • Any type of pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Mashrooms
  • Pesto sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta as regular
  2. Fry garlic and onion
  3. Add wshed and cut mashrooms
  4. Mix and wait for 10 minutes
  5. Add the cooked pasta in the pan with other ingredients
  6. Mix and add pesto sauce

Ginger cookies

Description

Winter ginger cookies

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Ginger powder
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix eggs with sugar using a mixer
  2. Add melted butter and mix
  3. Add baking powder and ginger powder
  4. Kneed the dough and roll it out
  5. Cut the dough into small circles
  6. Put them in the oven

Pumpkin soup


Description

A cream-soup with pumpkin

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin
  • Potatoes
  • Carrot
  • Pepper
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Slice pumpkin, potatoes, carrot, and pepper
  2. Put it in the boiling water
  3. Add a sliced onion
  4. Mix and wait till everything is soft
  5. Using a blender mix everything in a pan
